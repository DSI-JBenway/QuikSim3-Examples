# LC Steel Compression

Two-hit low-carbon steel compression program used to study grain refinement
through interpass dwell. The specimen is soaked at 1100 °C, hit to 30% strain,
held for 10 s, then hit again to an additional 40% strain before cooling.

## What it demonstrates

- Multi-hit deformation sequences
- Interpass dwell for recrystallisation studies
- Dilatometer acquisition channel for tracking phase transformations

## Key parameters

| Parameter        | Value       |
|------------------|-------------|
| Peak temperature | 1100 °C     |
| Soak time        | 60 s        |
| Hit 1 strain     | 0.3 @ 1 s⁻¹ |
| Interpass dwell  | 10 s        |
| Hit 2 strain     | 0.4 @ 1 s⁻¹ |
| Acquisition vars | TC1, Force, Stroke, Dilatometer |

## How to use

Open in QuikSim3 and **File → Save As** into your own project before editing.
